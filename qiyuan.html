公司概况
【业务范围】
新车进出口：提供全球知名品牌的新车进口与出口服务，涵盖轿车、SUV、商用车等多种车型。
二手车交易：经过严格检测的二手车资源，确保品质，满足不同客户的预算与需求。
汽车零部件供应：原装及适配零部件全球采购，快速响应客户需求。
定制化服务：根据客户需求提供车辆定制服务，包括外观设计、内饰配置及特殊功能定制等。
售后服务网络：建立全球化的售后服务体系，确保客户在全球范围内都能享受到便捷、高效的维修与保养服务。
技术支持与合作：与国内外知名汽车制造商、技术研发机构紧密合作，不断引入先进技术，提升产品竞争力。
[Scope of business]
New Car Import and Export: We provide new car import and export services for global famous brands, including cars, SUVs, commercial vehicles and other models.
Used Car Trading: Strictly tested used car resources to ensure quality and meet the budgets and needs of different customers.
Auto parts supply: original and adapted parts global procurement, rapid response to customer needs.
Customized Services: Provide customized services according to customer needs, including exterior design, interior configuration and special function customization.
After-sales service network: Establish a global after-sales service system to ensure that customers can enjoy convenient and efficient repair and maintenance services worldwide.
Technical Support and Cooperation: We work closely with well-known automobile manufacturers and technology research and development institutions at home and abroad to continuously introduce advanced technologies and enhance product competitiveness.
